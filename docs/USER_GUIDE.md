# LexMatePH — User guide

**LexMatePH** is a web app for Philippine law study: bar questions, Supreme Court decisions, codals, audio review, flashcards, and mock exams. This guide describes what each area does and how plans differ.

---

## Account and signing in

- Create an account or sign in with **Clerk** (Log In / Sign Up in the header or sidebar on mobile).
- Some features need an active subscription; others work on the free tier with limits.
- Your **plan** (tier) appears in the sidebar when you are signed in. Use **Upgrade** to change plans.

---

## How to navigate the app (step by step)

### Every screen

1. **Top bar** — Switch **Light** / **Dark** mode; open **Log In**, **Sign Up**, or your **profile** (avatar).
2. **Left sidebar** — Lists all main features. The active page is highlighted.
3. **Phone / small tablet** — Tap the **menu (☰)** icon in the top-left to open or close the sidebar. Tap outside the menu (dark overlay) to close it.

### Open a feature (from anywhere)

1. Open the **sidebar** (always visible on large screens; use **☰** on mobile).
2. Tap the name of the feature you want (see table below).
3. The **main area** switches to that feature. The sidebar item stays highlighted.

### Supreme Court (SC) Decisions

1. Sidebar → **SC Decisions**.
2. Use search, filters, or lists on the page to find a case.
3. **Tap a case** to open the detail view and read the digest.
4. Use **back** / navigation controls in that flow to return to the list (as the UI shows).

### Bar Questions

1. Sidebar → **Bar Questions**.
2. At the top of the page, open the **Bar subject** dropdown (**All subjects** or one subject).
3. Scroll the list; use **pagination** if your screen shows page controls.
4. Open a question card to read the prompt and suggested answer.

### LexCode (codals & statutes)

1. Sidebar → **LexCode**.
2. Use the **codal picker** (e.g. RPC, Civil Code, ROC, Constitution, Labor) at the top of the page.
3. Browse **articles or rules** in the reader; use in-page navigation (TOC, next/prev, or links) as provided.
4. Optional: use **fullscreen** from the LexCode toolbar if your build shows it, for distraction-free reading.

### Flashcards

1. Sidebar → **Flashcards**.
2. Wait until **“Loading SC concepts…”** finishes (first visit can take a moment). If you see an error, tap **Retry**.
3. On **Key legal concepts**, choose:
   - **All subjects** — deck from every available concept, or  
   - **One bar subject** tile — deck filtered to that subject only.
4. Your **flashcard session** starts; flip/advance with the on-screen controls.
5. To leave, use **Close** / back until you return to the main app (e.g. SC Decisions or sidebar).

### Lexify (mock Bar exams)

1. Sidebar → **Lexify**.
2. If your plan does not include Lexify, you will see an **upgrade** message — subscribe to unlock.
3. On the **dashboard**, pick an exam or paper and follow **Start** / on-screen steps (disclaimer, loading, lockdown if shown).
4. During the **live exam simulation**, the **LexMatePH header and sidebar hide** — only the exam UI is visible.
5. Submit when finished; open **Results**, then **return to dashboard** or exit Lexify via the app’s controls.

### LexPlay (audio study)

1. Sidebar → **LexPlay** (or use the control that opens the audio player, depending on your layout).
2. **Browse** codal or content from the player, **add tracks** to the queue, or load a **saved playlist** (sign in for playlists).
3. Use **Play / Pause**, **next / previous**, and volume as shown.
4. Optional: use **download / cache** actions in the player to save audio for **offline** listening when available.

### About & Updates

1. Sidebar → **About** — read product information.
2. Sidebar → **Updates** — read release notes or news posts.

## Main navigation (sidebar)

| Area | What it is |
|------|------------|
| **About** | Information about the app and the team. |
| **Updates** | News and changelog-style updates. |
| **Lexify** | Philippine Bar **mock exams** — timed sessions, secure simulator-style flow, results. |
| **Flashcards** | Study decks built from **key legal concepts** drawn from Supreme Court digests; filter by bar subject or study all. |
| **LexPlay** | **Listen** to codal and related content as **text-to-speech audio** — playlists, playback controls, optional **offline caching** for commuting. |
| **SC Decisions** | Browse **Supreme Court** case digests (search, open cases, read content). |
| **LexCode** | Read **codals and statutes** (e.g. RPC, Civil Code, Rules of Court, Constitution, Labor Code) in a reader built for law study. |
| **Bar Questions** | Browse **past bar exam questions** with suggested answers; filter by subject. |

On **desktop**, the sidebar stays visible. On **small screens**, open it with the **menu** icon in the top bar.

## Feature highlights

### Lexify (mock Bar exams)

- Choose a paper or flow from the **Lexify** dashboard and start a session.
- During an **active exam simulation**, the main app **header and sidebar hide** so you get a focused, full-area exam experience (like a secure browser window).
- After you submit, you can see **results** and return to the dashboard.
- **Subscription:** full Lexify access is tied to the **Barrister**-level plan (see Plans below).

### Flashcards

- Concepts are tied to **bar subjects** (e.g. Civil, Criminal, Labor). You can start a deck for **one subject** or **all subjects** when available.
- If concepts are still loading, wait for the list to finish; a slow first load can happen if the server is building a large list — retry or try again later.

### LexPlay (audio)

- Add items to a **queue**, use **saved playlists** when signed in, and control **play / pause / skip**.
- You can **cache audio for offline** listening from the player (subject to device and browser storage). Playback tries **local cache first**, then the network.

### SC Decisions

- Open a decision to read digest-style content; some views link to related **LexCode** or case tools depending on your plan.

### LexCode

- Pick a **codal** (e.g. RPC, Civil Code) from the page controls and navigate articles or rules in a reading layout.

### Bar Questions

- Use the **subject** filter on the page to narrow questions; pagination helps browse long lists.

## Plans and access (summary)

Plans are labeled in the app (e.g. **Free**, **Amicus**, **Juris**, **Barrister**). Typical pattern:

| Capability | Typical minimum plan |
|------------|----------------------|
| Stronger limits on digests, bar questions, flashcards, LexCode case links | **Amicus** and up |
| LexPlay listening and related extras | **Juris** and up (wording may vary in-app) |
| Case digest **download** (where offered) | **Juris** and up |
| **Lexify** mock exams | **Barrister** |

Exact rules and paywalls are enforced in the app when you try an action; the **Upgrade** flow explains current pricing and benefits.

---

## Tips

- **Signed in** unlocks playlists, LexPlay state sync, and other account features where supported.
- Use **Dark mode** if you study at night; preference is toggled in the header.
- If something fails to load, check your connection, then **refresh** the page. For LexPlay or heavy lists, try again after a short wait.

## Disclaimer

Study tools are for **education and self-assessment** only. They are not legal advice and are not affiliated with the Supreme Court of the Philippines or the Office of the Bar Confidant unless stated elsewhere in the product. Always verify materials against official sources for exams and practice.
